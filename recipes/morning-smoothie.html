Morning Smoothie Recipe

This delicious smoothie is my go-to breakfast when I'm looking to get a healthy start to my day!


Ingredients

  • 1 Large Banana
  • 1/2 Cup Frozen Blueberries
  • 1/2 Cup Frozen Raspberries (or Strawberries)
  • 1 Scoop Vanilla Protein Powder
  • 2 Tbsp Chia Seeds
  • 1/2 Cup Greek Yogurt
  • 1 Tbsp Coconut Oil (or Almond Butter)
  • 1-1 1/2 Cup Oat Milk
  • 1 Handful of Spinach or Kale

Steps

  1. Add frozen and fruit, protein powder, and chia seeds into the blender or blender cup first.
  2. Add banana, coconut oil, Greek yogurt, and spinach to blender.
  3. Pour in oat milk until max fill line is reached.
  4. Blend on high 30-45 seconds, adding more oat milk if smoothie is too thick.
  5. Enjoy!
