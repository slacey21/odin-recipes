Pão de Queijo

Pão de Queijo is a Brazilian cheese bread made with tapioca flour. These cheese breads are delicious and can frequently be found at Brazilian steakhouses!


Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 Cups (170g) Tapioca Flour
  • 2/3 Cup (160 mL) Milk
  • 1/3 Cup (80 mL) Oil
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1/2 Tsp Salt
  • 2/3 Cup (85g) Grated Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1/4 Cup (25g) Grated Parmesan Cheese
