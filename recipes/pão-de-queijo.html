Pão de Queijo

Pão de Queijo is a Brazilian cheese bread made with tapioca flour. These cheese breads are delicious and can frequently be found at Brazilian steakhouses!


Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 Cups (170g) Tapioca Flour
  • 2/3 Cup (160 mL) Milk
  • 1/3 Cup (80 mL) Oil
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1/2 Tsp Salt
  • 2/3 Cup (85g) Grated Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1/4 Cup (25g) Grated Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400˚F
  2. In a large bowl place tapioca flour. Set aside.
  3. In a medium saucepan place milk, oil, and salt. Bring to a boil. Add into tapioca flour and stir until combined. Add egg and stir until combined. Add grated cheeses and stir until incorporated and a sticky dough forms.
  4. Shape the dough into 1.5-inch balls and place on a parchment-lined baking sheet. Bake for 15-20 minutes, until lightly golden and puffed.
  5. Eat warm or let cool. Enjoy!
