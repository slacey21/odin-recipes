Buffalo Wings

Buffalo Wings are an American bar-food staple, and my favorite food!


Ingredients

  • 3lbs Chicken Wings, wings and drumettes split, wingtips removed (air-chilled chicken is best!)
  • 1 Tbsp Baking Powder
  • 1 Tsp Kosher Salt
  • 2 Tsp Garlic Powder

Buffalo Sauce

  • 1/4 Cup Unsalted Butter
  • 1/4 Cup Frank's Original Red Hot Sauce
  • 1 Tbsp Light Brown Sugar
